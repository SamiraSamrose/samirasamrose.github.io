About

I'm a SWE/AI engineer and ML practitioner based in Sunnyvale, California. I have completed my M.Sc. in Computer Science at Utah State University, Logan, UT, USA, thesis supervised by Dr. Curtis Dyreson and hold my B.Sc. in Computer Science & Engineering at Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology (AUST), Dhaka, Bangladesh, thesis advised by Syeda Shabnam Hasan.

Over 5+ years I've shipped production systems — from distributed backends handling billions of events to neural networks deployed at edge. I care obsessively about performance, clean architecture and the craft of writing code that lasts.

From zero to millions — I've led engineering teams, published research, contributed to open source and built products reaching over 5,000+ researchers, academic institutions and industry-level developers and end-users. When I'm not shipping, I'm attending at conferences, contributing to open source or writing about systems design.
